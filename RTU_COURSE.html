COURSES OFFERED:

Rajasthan Technical University offers various undergraduate degree courses namely , Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) ; Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch.) and Bachelor of Hotel Management and Catering Technology (BHMCT) . The various post graduate courses offered by University includes, Master of Technology (M.Tech.),Master of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Computer Applications (MCA). The list of affiliated Institute for the session 2018-2019 is under process.
